About Us - Gorewada Zoo

About Us

Gorewada Zoo, nestled in the heart of Nagpur, India, is a testament to our enduring commitment to wildlife conservation and education. Our journey began in 1990, with a vision to create a haven for diverse species and a platform for public awareness.

Over the years, Gorewada Zoo has evolved into a sprawling sanctuary, home to a myriad of fascinating creatures. From majestic tigers and playful bears to exotic birds and reptiles, our zoo offers a unique opportunity to witness the wonders of the natural world.

Beyond providing a delightful experience for visitors, Gorewada Zoo is actively involved in wildlife conservation and education. We participate in various breeding programs, research initiatives, and public awareness campaigns. Our goal is to inspire future generations to appreciate and protect our planet's biodiversity.

Join us on this wild adventure! Explore our lush forests, serene lakes, and vibrant enclosures. Witness the beauty and grace of our animals, and learn about their importance in the delicate balance of nature.

Together, let's create a brighter future for wildlife and humanity.
